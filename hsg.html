Our Story

Heartside Groves is a cozy guest house in the heart of coorg, created as a peaceful retreat to help guests escape, relax, and connect with nature. Founded in 2024 by Sanjana senthilkumar, our family-owned property is a labor of love, dedicated to genuine hospitality and comfort.

Our Mission

To provide an unforgettable retreat that blends comfort, sustainability, and local charm. Through personalized service, eco-friendly practices, and a passion for our community, we strive to create meaningful experiences that leave guests feeling refreshed, inspired, and connected.

Our Vision

To be a welcoming sanctuary where guests can experience the beauty of nature, warmth of hospitality, and comfort of a true home away from home.
